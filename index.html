What is Mask Network?

Mask Network brings privacy and benefits from Web3 to social media like Facebook & Twitter - with an open-sourced browser extension.

Using Mask Network

Install the Mask Extension

Login with Mask ID and connect your wallet

Use familiar Web 2.0 sites, enhanced with Web3 integrations

Encrypt Messages For Friends’ Eyes Only

Use the Mask Extension to hide your content from prying eyes and allow only your target audience to see it.

Launch New Projects Via a ITO (Initial Twitter Offering)

If you’re launching a new project on the Ethereum, BSC, Polygon, or Arbitrum networks, ITOs are a completely new way of launching decentralised assets fairly.

Built In Social Trading Tools

With integrated widgets on social media platforms, you’ll be able to view the latest prices of crypto assets without leaving the page. Powered by Uniswap, SushiSwap, Ox, and dHEDGE.

Verify NFT Avatars

Got a BAYC or a Punk? Get your profile pictures verified on-chain. For free.

Create Your Own Web 3 Profile

Aggregate your digital activities - including NFTs, POAPs, donation records etc - all in one place. Anonymised or not, that’s completely up to you.

Shop at Social Marketplaces

Buy and sell NFTs directly from social media platforms. Powered by OpenSea and other NFT marketplaces.

Start and Take Part In Giveaways

Gift NFTs and tokens to users on a first-come-first-served basis.

Store Your Files On a Decentralised Network

Use a next generation Dropbox. Permanent, uncensorable, open to all. Powered by Arweave.

On-ramp Fiat easily

Buy crypto assets directly via credit card or bank transfer in over 60 countries. Powered by Transak.
