Brand Guide

Use Maskbook logos in the right way.

Get Resources

Circle Avatar

The width of Maskbook logo should be 60% of the width of the circle canvas.

Background color should be #1C68F3. No drop shadow.

Square Avatar

The width of Maskbook logo should be 72% of the width of the circle canvas.

Background color should be #1C68F3. No drop shadow.

Free Canvas (Dark)

Simply put the white logo in the canvas.

Free Canvas (Light)

Use circle avatar or sqaure avatar instead.

If you use the square avatar on a free canvas, the border-radius of the square should be 10% of its width.

Text Logo

The text logo is made with Campton (Bold); no special letter-spacing.

The font is available for free downloads on MyFonts.

Shape-Text Combination

Vertical and horizontal combinations are usable.
